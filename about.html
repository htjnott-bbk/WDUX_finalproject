About Me

Biography

Hi, I'm Henry, I am a digital creative with four years of experience in the marketing industry. I've had experience working on a wide variety of projects, such as video production, graphics design, animation, display advertsting, website design and UX/UI analysis.

Since a young age, I have been fascinated by the impact of branding, the way in which products are marketed and how digital content influences our commercial behaviour. Whether that be cartoon characters on cereal boxes, social media and television advertising or an instantly recognisable font or typeface, recognisable visual elements always heighten the intrinsic value of the products that they are representing.
